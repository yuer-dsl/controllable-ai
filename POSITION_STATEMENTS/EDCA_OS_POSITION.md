# EDCA OS Position Statement within the Controllable AI Ecosystem

**Document Type:** System Position Statement  
**Status:** Frozen  
**Scope:** Architectural  
**Authority Level:** Informative (Non-Normative)

---

## 1. Purpose of This Document

This document defines the **precise position of EDCA OS**
within the broader **Controllable AI ecosystem**.

It exists to prevent:

- Over-claiming authority
- System-level centralization
- Misinterpretation of EDCA OS as a governance framework
- Confusion between specification and implementation

---

## 2. Core Position

> **EDCA OS is one possible operating system for implementing Controllable AI principles.**

It is:

- Not the definition of Controllable AI
- Not the entirety of Controllable AI
- Not a governance authority
- Not a regulatory framework

---

## 3. Functional Role

EDCA OS is designed to:

- Enforce explicit execution boundaries
- Structure AI reasoning into inspectable paths
- Preserve human responsibility at decision points
- Prevent implicit autonomous execution

Its function is **operational**, not normative.

---

## 4. Relationship to Controllable AI Specification

EDCA OS:

- Claims compatibility with the Controllable AI Specification
- Does not define or modify the specification
- May evolve independently
- May be replaced by other compliant systems
